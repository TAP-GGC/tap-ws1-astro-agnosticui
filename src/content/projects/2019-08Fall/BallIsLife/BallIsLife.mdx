Our project uses Sphero to teach programming fundamentals (for loops, functions, variables, etc.) in a hands on way.
We are incorporating basketball as a familiar medium to provide context to programming.
The students will navigate the Sphero through the basketball-themed obstacle course only using programming scripts to move the Sphero.

The Technology Ambassador Program at [GGC](http://www.ggc.edu/tap) strives to break the misconceptions of the IT field by providing fun workshops for students of all backgrounds. TAP students design engaging and fun outreach workshops to encourage interest in IT and STEM.
